## QT #15: July 2018 Webinar: The Chiral Heat Effect



Knowing is not enough; we must apply. Willing is not enough; we must do. “Do. Or do not. There is no try.”

**Action expresses the priorities of your choices at this moment. **

Consistently good in Nature will always beat intermittently perfect with technology and blue screens.

Action and attitude are linked more than your current circumstances. A happy person is not a person in a certain set of circumstances, but rather a person with a certain set of attitudes.
