## CPC #20: Geomagnetic Storms Mimic a 5G World



Solar storms bring space weather to the surface of the Earth.  The space environment cause humans a lot of problems.  For those who do not believe this just take a look at a human who spent a year in space and has an identical twin.  It is proof what nnEMF from the sun can do to us.  [Scott and Mark Kelly](http://www.livescience.com/62000-scott-kelly-dna-twin-study.html) are identical twin brothers — at least, they were until Scott spent a year living in space.
