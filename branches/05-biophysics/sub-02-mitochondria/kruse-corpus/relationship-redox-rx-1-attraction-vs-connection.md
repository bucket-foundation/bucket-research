## Relationship Redox Rx #1: Attraction VS. Connection



Very few people know the story of [Stevie Nix and her band mate](http://www.mtv.com/news/1609042/fleetwood-macs-stevie-nicks-dishes-on-her-relationship-with-lindsey-buckingam/).  She was Alannis Morisette before Alannis was.

Some people confuse intensity for passion and challenge for attraction.  This happens most often when people are not optimized biologically.
