## The Rx everyone needs but very few doctors write…



The mitochrondriac thought of the day from the garden in NOLA. Cancer is a profitable business to be in for hospitals. Have you ever seen how beautiful a hospital gardens are in their cancer wings. The landscaping is pristine, the garden furniture new and shiny and the flowers reflect gorgoeus rays of light for any eyes to see. I find it quite ironic that everytime I visit any hospital I never find any cancer patients out there. I never find their friends or family there either. I find hospital employees there on smoke breaks and lunch breaks. Cancer has to be a great business considering how elaborate these gardens are becoming in the modern hospital. You’d think that it would dawn on somebody that maybe sitting in these gardens might be wise for healing.
